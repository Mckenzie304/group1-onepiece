Facts about One Piece

  • The One Piece Wasn't Left By The Roger Pirates

    Unbeknownst to most citizens, the One Piece wasn't originally left by the Roger Pirates. At least some part of the treasure was planted by Joy Boy centuries before Monkey D. Luffy was born, adding to the mythos that surrounds the island of Laugh Tale.

  • Long Chapter

    Oda stated that One Piece chapter 597 is only the first half of the series.

  • Rich!

    The net worth of the One Piece franchise is over 20.9 billion USD. The Guinness Book of World Records recognized Oda for “most copies published for the same comic book series by a single author” in 2014. At that time One Piece sold over 320 million copies.

  • One Piece was only supposed to last for five years

    One Piece began 25 years ago, way before you were born! However, the author, Eiichiro Oda, said in the past that he originally planned to finish the story in 2002, after just five years. He even has the ending already planned out, but he has delayed it for many years as he continued to create epic characters, battles and adventures.

  • The End?

    One Piece is approaching its final saga, signaling that the end of the story is near. Fans can expect captivating storytelling and exciting events in the remaining arcs. In a Comicon interview, Oda confirmed that in the final arc of One Piece there will an character with an eye patch.
